Fancy Resturaunt Survival Guide

Many people have went to a resturaunt and had to deal with these serious issues

  • Ordered something completly different than what they thought
  • Used a dessert fork for a salad
  • Ordered from the "kids menu"
  • Declared bankruptcy

There are easy ways to avoid these situations and have a nice meal

Knowing what you ordered

Fancy eateries have their food written in a secret code, one must have mastered the arts of culinary to fully understand it. Learning this takes years so here are the basics

  • Scampi - a lemon butter sauce
  • Variety Meats - organs of an animal

This might seem like a little but it is better than knowing nothing

Using Correct utensils

Resturaunts use an essesive amount of utensils, cups, and plates to make it feel fancy, but if you use the wrong item for the wrong course, you will either ruin the plate or destroy your dish, use this diagram to know which item to use when.
